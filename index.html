About Us

LAROS MULTI INDOTEKNIK

PT Laros Multi Indoteknik is an integrator company specializing in Electrical, Instrument, Automation Total Solution, and Technical Services. We offers the best solutions for Industrial, Mining, Generating/Transmission/Power Distribution, building, and oil & gas sectors.
